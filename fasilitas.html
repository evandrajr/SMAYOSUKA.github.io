Fasilitas Sekolah

Library

Our library offers a vast collection of books and resources for research and leisure reading, providing a quiet space for learning and study.

Science Lab

Equipped with modern instruments and safety features, our science lab is designed to facilitate hands-on learning and experimentation.

Computer Lab

The computer lab is equipped with the latest technology to enhance digital literacy and support various tech-related projects.

Gymnasium

Our gymnasium provides facilities for various sports and fitness activities, promoting physical health and team spirit among students.

Art Room

The art room is equipped with materials and tools for creative expression, allowing students to explore their artistic talents.

Music Room

Our music room is designed for music education, complete with various instruments and equipment for practice and performance.

The cafeteria provides a range of nutritious meals and snacks, offering a comfortable place for students to socialize and recharge.

Auditorium

Our auditorium hosts various school events, including performances, assemblies, and guest lectures, equipped with advanced sound and lighting systems.

Playground

The playground is designed for recreational activities and physical exercise, providing a space for students to enjoy outdoor games and sports.

Health Clinic

Our health clinic is equipped to handle minor health issues and emergencies, ensuring the well-being of students throughout the school day.

Study Hall

The study hall provides a quiet space for students to work on assignments and study, promoting a productive learning environment.

Laboratory

The laboratory provides essential facilities for scientific experiments and research, supporting students' understanding of various scientific concepts.
