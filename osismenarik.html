Welcome to SMA Yos Sudarso Karawang

Welcome to our school! We are excited to have you join us on this journey of learning and growth. At SMA Yos Sudarso Karawang, we are committed to providing a nurturing and challenging environment where every student can achieve their full potential.

Modern Library

This library offers a serene and well-equipped environment for students to read, study, and conduct research. It is stocked with a wide range of books, digital resources, and comfortable seating areas.

Science Lab

Our state-of-the-art science lab provides students with hands-on learning experiences in physics, chemistry, and biology. It is equipped with modern tools and materials to foster curiosity and innovation.

Sports Complex

Our sports complex features a wide range of facilities, including a gymnasium, soccer field, and tennis courts, providing students with ample opportunities to engage in physical activities and sports.
